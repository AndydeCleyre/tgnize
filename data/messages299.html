Typically, As you know, DNS requests are sent in plain text format over port 53. Pihole and the likes can start a dns server, Intercept all incoming requests and filter them out.

In case of DoH, The client establishes a http2 connection to a DoH server like(https://cloudflare-dns.com/dns-query).
